## Purpose

This policy explains what information Erin Darrow ("we", "us", "our") at erindarrow.com collect, and how we use, store, and protect that information when you visit our site.

## Information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us via the site's contact form. When you use the contact form on this website, we may collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Any information you include in your message

We do not collect any sensitive personal information, and we do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this website beyond what is strictly necessary for it to function. The only kind of cookies we like are the edible ones.

## How we use your information

The information you provide through our contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry to respond to your message and follow up as appropriate.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not add you to any mailing lists without your explicit consent. We do not even have a mailing list, or marketing communications, to add you to.

## How we store your information

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party GDPR-compliant form handling service. Submissions are transmitted and stored on Formspree’s servers and delivered to us via email. You can review Formspree’s privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless otherwise required by law. We do not store your personal information in any other systems without your knowledge.

## Sharing your information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except where required by law or where necessary to respond to your enquiry.

## External links

This website contains links to third-party websites (including publishers, book sellers, social media, and others). Our privacy policy does not apply to externally linked third-party websites and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

##  Updates

This privacy policy may be updated in the future.

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please use the [contact form](https://erindarrow.com/contact/) to get in touch.
