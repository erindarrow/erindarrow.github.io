A Queen and six swans are not what they seem. The willow watches. The willow knows.

My dark fairy tale flash fiction **What the Willow Knows** is out now in the horror anthology _Once Upon a Moonless Night: Tales of Betrayal, Revenge, and Redemption_ from the [Brothers Uber](https://brothersuber.com/).

The anthology is available on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Once-Upon-Moonless-Night-Redemption-ebook/dp/B0GQ27FY11) in digital and print.
