Erin Darrow writes fantasy and science fiction inspired by nature, fairy tales, and folklore. When not writing, she enjoys wandering the woods, watching birds, caring for cats, and brewing herbal tea. Once upon a time, she was a scientist, mapmaker, and recordkeeper in ecology and earth science working to preserve knowledge and endangered species. She lives along the banks of the Waikato river in Aotearoa New Zealand.
<br><br>
Her stories appear in <i>DreamForge Magazine</i>, <i>Metaphorosis Books</i>, and others. You can also find her on <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/erindarrow">GoodReads</a> and <a href="https://bookstodon.com/@darrowerin">Mastodon</a>.
